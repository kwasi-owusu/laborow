How Tuetra Works

Rent nearby items. Earn from idle assets.

For Owners (List and Earn)

  1. Create an account and complete your profile.
  2. List your asset with photos, price, availability, and location area.
  3. Receive requests from renters and approve or decline.
  4. Hand over the asset based on the agreed time and conditions.
  5. Get paid and track rentals from your dashboard.
  6. Confirm return and leave a review to build trust.

For Renters (Find and Rent)

  1. Create an account and set your location to discover nearby items.
  2. Search and filter by category, distance, availability, and price.
  3. Request to rent and wait for the owner’s approval.
  4. Complete payment (if required) and confirm pickup.
  5. Use responsibly and return the item on time.
  6. Rate your experience to help the community.

Trust and Safety

Tuetra encourages responsible use and respectful communication. Always confirm rental terms clearly, keep conversations professional, and report suspicious activity through support.
